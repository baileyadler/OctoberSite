Impact

On May 21, 1881, Clara Barton established the American Red Cross Foundation. She was president of this organization for more than 23 years!

Clara Barton was involved in the federal government through the Patent Office. This was an unique opportunity as she advocated that women should receive equal salaries as men.

During 1905, Clara Barton founded the National First Aid Association of America. She provided first aid kits and taught basic medical skills to people around the world.
