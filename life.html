Life

She was born in Massachusetts on December 25, 1821 and had four siblings. She was homeschooled by her siblings

After the American Civil War, Clara Barton spoke about her experience with providing supplies to the soldiers on the Battlefield. She suffered from double pneumonia and died in April 12, 1912

During 1905, Clara Barton founded the National First Aid Association of America. She provided first aid kits and taught basic medical skills to people around the world.
